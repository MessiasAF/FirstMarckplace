My History

An Artist Rose from the Ashes

    There was a time when I stopped drawing, driven away by the demands of school and life. However, my passion for art never completely died. Years later, I decided to pick up the pen and paper again, and this time, with more determination than ever.

A New Era

    Armed with a digitalizing table, I began to produce digital paintings, but my passion soon faded. However, recently, I decided that it was time to start for real. Although I had no technique, perspective or advanced skills, I started studying and practicing, and the result is evident.

The Journey of an Artist

    There's nothing more rewarding than seeing one's own progress, and that's what I'm doing now. I continue to study, improve and produce content for you, and my goal is to make art my profession.

The Future is Now

    I thank each of you who have already supported me, and I invite those who want to help me achieve my dream to visit my NFTs and drawing courses available on my website. Whether you're learning to draw cartoons or flowers, your contribution will be immensely valuable to me.

Together We are Stronger

    I remember that small actions are already a great help, but if you want to be part of something bigger, consider purchasing one of my products. I am counting on your generosity and support to make my dream a reality. Let's go together, with determination and passion, towards success.
